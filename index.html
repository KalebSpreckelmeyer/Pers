About Me

My name is Kaleb,

I’m a computer programmer with an interest in web development. I got my start at Reynolds Community College in Virginia with an Associates in Information Systems Technology. Now, I am practicing in my spare time in preparation for a Bachelors of Computer Science at Missouri Science and Technology in the upcoming fall semester of 2023.

I’m always excited to learn something new or to innovate in any form, and creating sites like this push me towards that goal. I hope to one day be like my college professors back at Reynolds. They were so knowledgeable you could ask them an obscure question about a language they consider themselves weak in and they’ll be right back to you with an in-depth and thoughtful response. I want to be like that, not only for myself but for others.

I would like nothing more than to end my career in a college classroom sharing the knowledge and experiences I’ve acquired throughout my years, but the first step in that process is acquiring and practicing with that knowledge. This site was one small step towards that marathon of a goal, and I hope to make many more.
